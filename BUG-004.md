# 🐛 BUG-004 — Cart Accepts Zero and Negative Quantity

## Summary
The cart quantity field accepts 0 and negative values, making the total price ₹0 or negative.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Add a product to cart
2. Change quantity to `0` or `-1`
3. Click Update

## Expected Result
Error: *"Quantity must be at least 1"* or item removed from cart.

## Actual Result
Cart updates with 0 quantity, total becomes ₹0.

## Impact
Critical business logic failure. Users could order items for free.
