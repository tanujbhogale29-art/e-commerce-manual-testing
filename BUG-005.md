## Summary
When the quantity of a product is changed in the cart, the total price does not update automatically.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Add a product to cart (e.g., ₹500)
2. Change quantity from 1 to 9999
3. Click Update

## Expected Result
Total price = 9999 × ₹500 = ₹49,99,500

## Actual Result
Total still shows ₹500 (original price, not updated).

## Impact
Incorrect pricing shown to user. Can cause revenue loss or customer disputes.
